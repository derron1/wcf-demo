FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

CAN I MEET THE STUDENTS I SUPPORT?

[+]

Yes, we organize annual events where donors can meet grant recipients and hear about their educational journey and achievements.

IS MY DONATION TAX-DEDUCTIBLE?

[+]

Yes, as a registered charity in the UK, your donations are eligible for Gift Aid which allows us to claim an extra 25p for every Â£1 you donate.

WHAT'S THE MINIMUM DONATION AMOUNT?

[+]

There is no minimum donation amount. Every contribution, no matter how small, helps us support women's education.

CAN I SPECIFY HOW MY DONATION IS USED?

[+]

Yes, you can specify if you'd like your donation to support specific fields of study or particular programs within our foundation.

HOW DO YOU SELECT SCHOLARSHIP RECIPIENTS?

[+]

Recipients are selected through a rigorous application process based on financial need, academic merit, and commitment to their chosen field of study.
