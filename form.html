‚ùå We DO NOT Fund:

  • Degree courses - Undergraduate, Postgraduate, Masters, or PhDs
  • ALL language courses - Including English (ESOL, EFL, IELTS) and any foreign languages
  • Courses below Level 3 - Any Level 1 or Level 2 qualifications
  • Courses already started - You must NOT have begun the course before applying
  • Hobby or recreational courses - Must be career/employment focused
  • Courses outside the UK - Must be delivered within the United Kingdom
  • Courses without vocational outcomes - Must lead to employment/career advancement
  • General interest courses - Must have clear professional purpose

‚ö†Ô∏è Important: Your course MUST be Level 3 or above AND have clear employment outcomes to be eligible for funding.

‚úÖ Examples of What We DO Fund (Level 3+):

  • Level 3 Vocational qualifications - NVQ Level 3, BTEC Level 3, City & Guilds Level 3+
  • Professional certifications - AAT Level 3/4, CIPD Level 3+, PRINCE2, etc.
  • Advanced trade skills - Level 3 plumbing, electrical installation, beauty therapy
  • IT and digital skills - Professional coding bootcamps, certified web development, data analysis
  • Healthcare training - Level 3+ care qualifications, phlebotomy, dental nursing
  • Business skills - Professional bookkeeping (Level 3+), project management, marketing
  • Creative industry training - Professional design courses, certified photography, etc.

‚úì Remember: All funded courses must be Level 3 or above, UK-based, and lead directly to employment opportunities.
