Supporting Women's Education Since 1900

The Women's Careers Foundation is committed to supporting women who seek to achieve their career goals through education and vocational training. If you are interested in learning more about volunteering opportunities with the Foundation, please get in touch with us by using our Contact Form.

SOCIAL EVENTS

We hold occasional social events where past, current and potential grant recipients can meet us and one another to network and share information and experiences. Members and grant recipients past and present are warmly invited to our AGM and to contribute to the future of the charity.
